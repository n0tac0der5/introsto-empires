About Mahaya

Mahaya Boicel

Curated Business Matchmaker & Strategic Consultant

With over two decades of experience in business management, customer service, promotion, and entrepreneurial leadership, Mahaya Boicel brings a rare combination of emotional intelligence, street-savvy strategy, and visionary insight to the world of business matchmaking and consulting.

Mahaya began her career building and managing businesses across the wellness, creative, and service industries. Her journey includes founding and operating a successful health and wellness clinic in Montreal, where she developed a reputation for connecting people across industries and creating growth opportunities through collaboration. After closing the clinic, Mahaya transformed her setback into a rebirth—refining her natural gift for forging meaningful business relationships and curating strategic partnerships that produce real results.

Now, as a Curated Business Matchmaker, Mahaya leverages her instinctive people-reading skills and wide-reaching network to connect entrepreneurs, creatives, investors, and organizations with the right allies to move their visions forward. She works across a range of industries—including tech, blockchain, music and entertainment, and health and wellness—bringing cross-sector insight and a deep understanding of how innovation, culture, and community intersect.

Her services extend into strategic business consulting, helping clients refine their positioning, align their values with their business models, and build strategies that reflect both purpose and profitability. Her lived experience, from personal reinvention to business revival, adds depth to her counsel and authenticity to her voice.

A powerful communicator and sought-after moderator and panelist, Mahaya is known for facilitating honest dialogue, asking the right questions, and drawing out the deeper motivations that drive founders, creatives, and changemakers.

Whether in the boardroom, on stage, or behind the scenes, Mahaya operates with clarity, warmth, and a bold commitment to excellence. She is currently focused on expanding her work internationally, particularly within the Afro-diaspora, creative economies, and emerging entrepreneurship hubs.
